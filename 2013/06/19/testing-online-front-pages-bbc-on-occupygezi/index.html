Testing online front pages in #pageonex: BBC coverage on #occupygezi

Posted on June 19, 2013 by pablo | Leave a reply

PageOneX, as it is now, doesn’t support online newspaper front pages, but I decided to give it a try using the recently discovered pastpages.org archive. I used it to track the coverage of he #occupygezi protests in Turkey by the BBC.

Maybe is it better to use only the “above” the fold (first screenshot)? In his case the result is pretty much the same.
